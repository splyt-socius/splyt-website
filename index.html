How it works

Splyt is a two-sided model that brings together club-goers and club owners alongside a seamless payment provider to democratize access to VIP services.

Splyt allows club-goers to create digital lobbies for private sections & bottle service orders. Club-goers may choose to keep their lobby private and invitation only, or they may open the lobby up to the public, enabling them to meet new friends while lowering the cost.
