Why Splyt?

Have you ever wanted to purchase a private section at a club and encountered a setback due to not being able to individually afford the cost of a reservation? or simply think it is not worth purchasing a section with so much vacancy you probably would not use? Splyt helps you create lobbies that allow you split (get it?) the cost of acquiring such private sections.

How it works

We bring together club goers, club owners, and a seamless payment provider. The Splyt algorithm is 2-side facing for club goers and clubs with available private section for purchase.

A club goer may create a private or a public lobby. Most users would benefit a lot from public lobbies as they allow sole parties to find and join existing lobbies and evenly split the cost of a section.

Our mission

While we are currently focused on improving how private sections are
purchased in clubs through the use of user generated lobbies and payment
splits, we aspire to broaden our horizon to a lot more shared payment use
cases as the world's reliance on technology for basic activities keeps evolving.

Our ultimate goal is to reduce the barrier to entry for large individual purchases.
